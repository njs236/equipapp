PAUL DALE/CHURCH BY THE BRIDGE/SYDNEY, AUSTRALIA/Paul Dale is the senior pastor of Church by the Bridge in Sydney. Church by the Bridge started in 2005 with 30 people, and under God is now a church in 7 locations. In everything, they have sought to be a church that ‘lives for Jesus and loves like Jesus’. Paul is married to Rachel and they have 3 boys. Paul will be teaching us from the book of James.
DAVE CLANCEY/ST SAVIOURS AND ST NICHOLAS ANGLICAN CHURCH, CHRISTCHURCH/Dave Clancey is the senior pastor of Trinity South Christchurch. He is married to Amanda and they have three sons – Liam, Theo, and Caleb. Dave will be teaching us from the life of David recorded for us in the books of 1 and 2 Samuel.
